San Francisco Transportation Noise Plan

Many of the adverse impacts of transportation noise can be limited through sound urban planning. The Transportation Noise Plan, which is part of the City's General Plan, lists city policies to reduce transporation nosie and its impacts on health and welfare.

WHO Guidelines for Community Noise

The WHO Community Noise Guidelines consolidate scientific knowledge on the health impacts of community noise and provide community standards to protect people from the harmful effects of noise in non-industrial environments.

U.S Noise Control Act

The U.S. Noise Control Act gave Federal agencies the authority to set standards on products that produce noise. Since 1982, the EPA has not had funding to implement the Act.
