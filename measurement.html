How To Take a Noise Measurement Using a Mobile App

Typically, Type 1 or 2 noise meters can cost upwards of $100, but several free or inexpensive mobile device apps now allow residents to take community noise measurements. Testing by scientists from the National Institute for Occupational Safety and Health has found several of the apps to be highly accurate.
We provide instructions for using one of these apps, the SPLnFFT Noise Meter for iOS, below:

Using the SPLnFFT Noise Meter App

1. Open up the SplFFT app

2. Point the phone microphone towards the source of the noise.

3. On the meter dashboard, change the response time from “FAST” (default) to “SLOW.”

4. At the bottom of the app, set the frequency response selector to “dBA”.

5. Hold the RESET button down for one second. This will clear the memory and reset the timer.

6. Record the noise level for at least 60 seconds.

7. Touch the “LIVE” button at the top left hand side of the screen. This will pause the noise recording.

8. Note the Leq level, the Max level, and the duration of the recording, which is seen at the bottom right hand corner of the app.
